Volumetric World

In the academic project, inspiration was drawn from M.C. Escher's 'Relativity' and Greek architecture. This undertaking involves the creation of a compact volumetric world where geometric designs and a vocabulary of shapes are explored. Within this spatial construct, pathways are crafted to guide the observer through an immersive experience, blending the surreal dimensions of Escher's work with Greek architectural aesthetics.
