Osso

Osso is a website with BackOffice elaborated in an academic context based on the Osso Magazine and its content.
On the website, the user can browse through the various editions of Osso Magazine and its articles. A 3D element, shaped like a bone (osso in portuguese), was developed to accompany the user during his navigation through the website.
Academic Project — MDM 2022 Collaboration with Laura Pais and Francisca Calisto Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WordPress
