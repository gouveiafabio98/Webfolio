GeoAtlântico Game

I was responsible for the full development of GeoAtlântico, a web-based educational game designed for both desktop and mobile. The game was launched in February 2025 as part of the “Rotas do Património” initiative promoted by AD ELO, with the goal of valuing and disseminating the geo-patrimony and cultural heritage of central Atlantic Portugal.
GeoAtlântico is an interactive quiz game focused on the Bairrada and Mondego region, covering the municipalities of Cantanhede, Figueira da Foz, Mealhada, Mira, Montemor-o-Velho, and Penacova. It features multiple-choice questions presented through an illustrated interactive background and a roulette-based question selection system.
The game includes two play modes—Classic (untimed) and Challenge (30 seconds per question)—and was built to be accessible, intuitive, and engaging for users of all ages. It is available online in free access and serves both educational and outreach purposes, promoting geoeducation, geotourism, and cultural awareness.
