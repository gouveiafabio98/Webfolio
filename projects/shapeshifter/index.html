Shapeshifter

Throughout history, mythical creatures have sparked our imagination. Shapeshifter is designed to harness the power of this imagination, allowing artists, regardless of their 3D modeling experience, to create innovative and usable 3D creatures.
Shapeshifter seamlessly integrates into Blender, the open-source 3D modeling platform, making it accessible to a thriving community of 3D modelers. The tool unlocks the creative process by offering a controlled generation of original creatures.
Ensuring credibility in creature combinations is key. Shapeshifter empowers users to manipulate and control the impact of this vital component, resulting in a cohesive and realistic blend of creature elements, both geometrically and texturally.
The workflow and detailed tool explanation can be found in my Master's thesis document, which delves into the project's inception, context, goals, and the methodology adopted. It also explores the state of the art in 3D element construction, texturing, and credibility within the realm of creature creation.
The Shapeshifter addon is available for download on GitHub.
