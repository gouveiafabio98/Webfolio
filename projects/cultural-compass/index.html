Cultural Compass

Crimes Exemplares (Exemplary Crimes) is a literary work, composed of small texts, that report various crimes, in the most varied forms and situations. Mostly surreal in nature. These criminal acts have impulses of extreme violence, without an ethical or moral nature, and, with its sort of comic speech, it enhances the absurd.
Two transmedia objects of this stories were developed, a board game and an augmented reality poster. In both the objects developed, these contrasting dualities were explored: the sobriety and the madness, the animal and the innocent, the veracity and the unbelief, supported by elements that create a connection to the crimes.
This two objects were displayed at the exhibition Formas: do Texto Tangível ao Imaterial in Convento de São Francisco, Coimbra (from November 7th, 2021 to January 2nd, 2022).
