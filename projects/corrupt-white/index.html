Corrupt White

Corrupt white is a visual experience of feeling exploration ignited by interaction with your mobile device.
Each feeling tries to reproduce itself through movement, always altering it in different ways. This is an academic project made by students of multimedia design at the faculty of science and technology in university of coimbra, portugal.
Academic Project — LDM Collaboration: Laura Pais and Marcelo Coelho Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, WebSockets, p5.js, Node.js, mySQL
