crimeStage

CrimeStage is an academic project prototype that transforms the classic Mafia game into an interactive experience played on a multitouch table. It serves as both the game board and narrator. Players use their smartphones to access the virtual game room, where they are assigned roles as hotel guests or killers. By following the table's instructions, they investigate displayed objects for clues, engage in discussions, and cast votes through the table's interface. The game proceeds until there are no more killers or they outnumber the guests, offering a distinctive fusion of social deduction and interactive gaming.

Academic Project — MDM 2022 Collaboration: Alexandra Oliveira, Inês Pinto and Marcelo Coelho Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WebSockets, Node.js
